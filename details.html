Sub Sonic

Backstory: Sub Sonic graduated first in his class at the Superhero Academy, known for his exceptional speed reaching around 1,225km per hour or Mach 1, and an ability to project his voice, hence the hero name Sub Sonic.

Aqua Beast

Backstory: Aqua Beast graduated first in his class at the Hero Academy, known for his exceptional ability to manipulate water and communicate with aquatic life, hence the hero name Aqua Beast. Grew up near the coast and loved to swim in the ocean.

Shadow Boom

Backstory: Shadow Boom graduated first in his class at the Hero College, known for her exceptional ability to manipulate shadows and move at superhuman speeds, hence the hero name Shadow Boom. She Loves the night, usually found staring into the night sky when she was little. Loved to play hide and seek.

Thunder Strike

Backstory: Thunder Strike graduated first in his class at the Lightning Academy, known for his exceptional ability to manipulate lightning and fly, hence the hero name Thunder Strike. Grew up in a small town and was fascinated by thunderstorms. Would often play in the rain and was always drawn to the sound of thunder.

Gallexus

Backstory: Gallexus graduated first in his class at the 5 Pillars Academy, known for his exceptional strength, speed, durability, flight, super senses and enhanced intelligence. Though he didnt grow up with powers, he was gifted by the 5 Pillars themselves, who saw his potential and gave him their blessing. Following their guidance, he became a powerful hero in their service. Eventually becoming the leader of the 5 Pillars. Also known as the First Pillar.
